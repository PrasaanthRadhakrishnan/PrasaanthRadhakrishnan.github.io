Overview of Electronics

The field of electronics is a branch of physics and electrical engineering that deals with the emission, behaviour and effects of electrons using electronic devices. Electronics have an application in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, healthcare, and telecommunications. Developing electronic circuits may include creating schematics and circuit diagrams, and selecting and sourcing the necessary components. Once the design is complete, the electronics are built and tested to ensure that they function as intended.

This encompasses a wide range of areas including circuit design, microprocessors, digital signal processing, and telecommunications.

My Experience:

The first time I worked on building basic electronic circuits such as sound amplifiers and PCB design. The programming for this was done n C++.
